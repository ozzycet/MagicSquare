Magic Squares

A magic square is a square grid of numbers where the sums of the numbers in each row and each column are all the same. This special total is called the magic sum (or magic constant). Magic squares usually use consecutive whole numbers starting from 1.

They have been studied for hundreds of years and appear in math, puzzles, and even art. Magic squares are interesting because they combine simple numbers with surprising patterns and balance.
